About Us

We are the Entrepreneurship and Investment Club of IIITD&M Kancheepuram. We aim to inculcate the spirit of business, entrepreneurship, management and investments among our fellow IIITD&M students.

What We Do.

  • Informative Seminars - Hold club sessions to share information about current trends in the entrepreneurship and investments world. Topics like digital currencies, how to make money in college et al.

  • The STARTUP Scene - We hold sessions to tell people about the current startup trends in India, and have discussions and debates on what makes a startup successful, or what brings its downfall. We are no experts, but we make best efforts to learn.

  • And more to come in the near future!
