What is Project Kandigai ?

TLDR; Come up with a strategy to convince vendors and shopkeepers to give discounts to IIITians.

It is a 7 day long event (13th - 20th Oct) held by EPIC as part of Cluster Events. The participants form teams of size 2 to 4. Each team is expected to reach out to a unique Shop in Kandgai , Mambakkam , Kelambakkam or Vandaloor and strike a Deal with the business owner on behalf of the student community of IIITDM.

The deal maybe a weekly delivery of Goods directly to IIITDM on demand , or something as simple as a reasonable discount on a Haircut from a saloon near by. The team that strikes the best deal wins.

Rules of the Game

Be polite

1 Be polite to the business owners.(Very very Important). Remember that they are doing us a favour and getting a favour in return. Do not indulge in any sort of agrument with the shop keepers or any other people.

Proof of Concept

2 Please make sure to record all your conversation in the form of a video or an audio format. The recording can be take by phone. Please ensure that the shopkeepers are aware of the fact that you are recoding the conversation. Video recordings get more points :P

Its Unofficial

3 Please remember that you do not represent IIITDM in any Official way. All offers , conversations and deals are purely unofficial. There are no contracts to sign and no lawyers to consult.

Be reasonable

4 Make sure that the offers and the discounts that you ask for are viable for the shopkeepers too. Do not ask for huge discounts. Remember that the ultimate Goal is the make a lasting and healthy relationship between the student community and the businesses around IIITDM so that both parties can benifit.
